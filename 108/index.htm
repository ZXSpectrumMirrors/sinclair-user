Sinclair User would like to express its deepest regrets at the recent death of Mike Johnston, who was the Consultant Editor on the initial launch of Sinclair User. He was first secretary of the Guild of Software Houses and was always a driving force within the industry. He will be remembered for his tireless work running ZX Microfairs which he himself initiated and his loss will be felt by everyone that had contact with him.
