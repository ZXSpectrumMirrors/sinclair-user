Sinclair User is published monthly by ECC Publications Ltd. It is not in any way connected with Sinclair Research Ltd.

  • SINCLAIRVOYANCE We look at the difficulties caused by rising expectations in the home computer market.

  • SINCLAIR USER CLUB The latest happenings in our growing user group, and two more exclusive offers.

  • NEWS Sales of the ZX-81 revive, another rival in the home computer market, copyright problems, and much more.

  • YOUR LETTERS More news from our readers about what is happening in the Sinclair market.

  • FUTUROLOGY Bill Martin and Sandra Mason look at patterns of leisure in the future.

  • SOFTWARE SCENE Various forms of shooting, fighting and blasting dominate the software reviews this month.

  • HARDWARE WORLD We look at more of the ingenious devices which have been produced to expand the uses of your Sinclair machines.

  • INSIDE SINCLAIR Jim Westwood has been with Clive Sinclair since his earliest days. Claudia Cooke speaks to him.

  • BUSINESS SOFTWARE John Gilbert looks at the growing library of programs for the more serious user.

  • MACHINE CODE Mike Biddell answers some of the queries which arose from his earlier series on the subject.

  • STARTING FROM SCRATCH Our regular article aimed at those who have joined the world of Sinclair users recently.

  • BOOKS John Gilbert takes some more publications off the shelf.

  • PROGRAM PRINTOUT A variety of uses are covered in our regular eight pages of programs from our readers.

  • COMPETITION WINNER The winner of our July contest is from Stranraer and chose a serious application for his 48K program.

  • HELPLINE Andrew Hewson provides solutions for more of your problems.

  • MIND GAMES Philip Joy assesses the interest of more games.

  • FREE INSIDE - Spectrum User
