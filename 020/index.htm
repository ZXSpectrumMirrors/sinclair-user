Sinclair User is published monthly by ECC Publications Ltd.

  • SINCLAIRVOYANCE Are there signs that the micro bubble may have burst?

  • SINCLAIR USER CLUB More offers and news about the biggest club for Sinclair users in the country.

  • NEWS Record profit for Sinclair Research; sales of ZX-81 to China; and much more.

  • SINCLAIR SIMON Our hero wonders about the Chinese ZX-81.

  • LETTERS We choose the best from our bumper postbag.

  • HARDWARE WORLD Modems and interfaces dominate the new add-ons.

  • SOFTWARE SCENE Our writers review the latest releases.

  • COMPILERS John Gilbert assesses the software packages which allow programmers to turn slow Basic programs into much faster listings.

  • MICRONET ADAPTOR Stephen Adams looks inside the little box which allows you to contact large databases.

  • MICRODRIVE John Gilbert speaks to Dr Ian Logan who was responsible for much of the work on the ROM of the new add-on.

  • PROGRAM PRINTOUT Sixteen pages of the best listings sent by our readers.

  • USER OF THE MONTH A Canterbury schoolmaster tells Nicole Segre how he revolutionised administration at his school.

  • FORTH John Gilbert continues his series on the alternative language, reporting on the importance of the Editor.

  • PROGRAMMING John Armfield shows how to display results from programs in the best way.

  • HELPLINE Andrew Hewson answers your problems and gives some hints on improving your programming.

  • BOOKS More technical information is being published but John Gilbert thinks that there is room for improvement.

  • STARTER PACK In our new expanded section we include an article on error-trapping techniques.

  • MIND GAMES Quentin Heath travels to the Wild West to search for gold.

  • SOFTWARE DIRECTORY This month we include our list of cassettes in full.
