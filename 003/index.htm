Sinclair User is published monthly by ECC Publications Ltd. It is not in any way connected with Sinclair Research Ltd.

  • SINCLAIRVOYANCE We consider the effects the new ZX Spectrum could have on the add-on market for the ZX-81.

  • SOFTWARE SCENE We look once more at the growing library of programs.

  • HARDWARE WORLD Stephen Adams assesses the latest add-ons to reach the market.

  • LETTERS We print another selection of your letters from our growing postbag.

  • NEWS Two winners of the Prestel adaptor competition, Sinclair Research year-end results, and more.

  • SPECTRUM REVIEW An in-depth assessment of the capabilities of the Sinclair Research addition to its range of computers.

  • MOTHER'S LAMENT Nicole Segre tells how her son's new ZX-81 changed life for her family.

  • ADVENTURE GAMES Phil Garrett continues to battle his way through catacombs, Inca temples and nasty mountains.

  • GRAPHICS Illustrations can help to retain people's attention and add to their enjoyment. We look at some of the systems on offer.

  • COMPETITION WINNER We profile the winner of our first competition, a teacher from Wellingborough, Northamptonshire.

  • PROGRAMS Another eight pages of programs for you to try.

  • BOOKS We look at some more of the many books which have been published to help people to understand how to use their Sinclair machines and what they can do.

  • INSIDE SINCLAIR We interview Nigel Searle, the recently-appointed head of the computer division of Sinclair Research and discover his plans for the company expansion.

  • MACHINE CODE The second in our series telling you how you can learn to use machine code to increase the enjoyment of using the ZX-81.

  • MIND GAMES Philip Joy looks at the simple game of Nim and ways of making it more interesting.

  • EXP AND LN Ian Stewart makes another trip into the unexplored regions of the ZX-81 keyboard.

  • HELPLINE Andrew Hewson with tips and hints on how to make better use of your Sinclair computer.

  • SINCLAIR USER CLUB We start a new club for readers of Sinclair User with many special benefits.

  • COMPETITION We have a special competition this month and offer a ZX Spectrum and printer as the prize.

  • ADVERTISEMENT INDEX Due to popular demand, we have started to include an index to help you find the advertisements you specifically want.
