  • SINCLAIRVOYANCE Clive Sinclair makes his excuses for delays in Spectrum delivery. The product may be good but production could be better.

  • SINCLAIR USER CLUB More news on the club scene.

  • NEWS Software and hardware prices start to fall; the designers of the Spectrum branch-out on their own.

  • YOUR LETTERS You give us your views on the world of Sinclair computers.

  • GOLDEN AGES Clive Sinclair gives his views on the future.

  • SOFTWARE SCENE The invaders are let loose on the Spectrum and the mysteries of the Sinclair Horizons give-away are probed.

  • STARTING FROM SCRATCH Our guide to how to learn to use your ZX-81.

  • PROGRAM PRINTOUT Eight more pages of our popular programs.

  • TAPE RECORDERS AND COMPUTERS Mike Salem gives hints on tape storage.

  • HELPLINE Andrew Hewson answers readers' questions.

  • COMPETITION WINNER The winner of our EPROM blower competition.

  • COMPANY PROFILE Data-Assette entered the ZX-81 market via its tape business. It now sees its future tied closely to the machine.

  • MIND GAMES Philip Joy explores chess, one of the oldest mind games.

  • FREE INSIDE - Spectrum User: devoted to news about the Spectrum, its uses and its problems.
