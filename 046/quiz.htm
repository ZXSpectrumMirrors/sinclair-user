Quiz


Gremlin's
Festive Trivia Test

SICK of Christmas parties? Fed up with New Year's Eve knees-ups? Feel the urge to scream 'grosso mundi' at the latest dried turkey sinew sandwich?

Relax, gentle punters, with Gremlin's Festive Trivia Test. Better still, forge your own Trivial Pursuits cards and then you too can join in the traditional holiday aprés blow-out activities with a chance of winning for a change.

Gremlin has ransacked his dusty files to provide searching questions to test your knowledge of gossip, hype and the duller parts of obscure games ... Most answers have appeared one way or the other in Sinclair User over the past year, but a few you'll never get, not ever.

After all, who says Gremlin has to be fair? Where does it say that in writing? Is it a law, yet? Is it ... (Back to your cave, vile beastie ... Ed)
